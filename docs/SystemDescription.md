System description V1.0

**Purpose

We are building a power company that brokers energi from https://energinet.dk/el/ and sells it with a tiny added price per kwh and a small subscription fee. The goal for this project is to build the basic infrastructure to be able to run such a company. 

**Customers

Our customers are regular people subscribing to our power service and getting power brokered by us. The customer will need to be able to sign up for the service and be onboarded as a customer. When they become customers they need to be able to access their account through a companion app. 

In the companion app they can see the current rate per KWH and they can see their current bill. 

For payment they can pay only with Mobile Pay subscriptions. This happens monthly or weekly depending on preferences. 

**Administration 

As a employee with the company you need to be able administrate the customer base. You need to view customers as a whole how many, current income, margins per customer, margins in total. You also need to look at overall expenses to power broker. 

Exact features towards the power broker will have to depend on further research tasks. 

A web site is a preferred platform for administration. 

**Marketing

There is a need for a marketing site where potential customers can read about the service. New customers can sign up from the landing page and become customers immediately. 

**Design

Look and feel is green and light. Use latest design trends in web and liquid glass for iOS. Emphasize simpel and functional. It has to be incredibly easy to become a customer and to sign up. 

**Research 

We need to research open apis to gather information on power prices live from the broker. Include how to broker power for 3rd parties and the cost involved.

We need to research how the customer access their information on their current subscription. The authentication method will be with MitID.

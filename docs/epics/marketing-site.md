## Overview

This component delivers the public Marketing Site for The Cheap Power Company: a responsive, server-rendered, Danish-localised (da-DK) website that explains the offering, displays a live spot-price widget, links out to the Onboarding Flow signup, and provides the legal/informational pages required for launch. The critical path is Epic 1 (project setup) blocking everything; Epic 6 (price widget) and Epic 4 (signup CTA flow) provide contracts consumed by Epic 2 (landing page); Epic 5 (supporting pages) ships the footer required on every page; Epic 7 (performance & SEO) and Epic 8 (error states) are final hardening passes. The marketing site never calls Energi Data Service or energinet.dk directly — all live price data comes from the internal backend price API per ADR-001 §3.4.
